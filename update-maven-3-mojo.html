wagon:update-maven-3

Full name:

org.codehaus.mojo:wagon-maven-plugin:1.1-SNAPSHOT:update-maven-3

Description:

Work around for WAGON-407 to copy commons-io, commons-lang, and jsoup to ${maven.home}/lib/ext directory.
